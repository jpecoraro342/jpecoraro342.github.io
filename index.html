Portfolio

Personal Projects

Risky Business

Risky Business is a turn based strategy game for android. It combines the strategic gameplay of Settlers of Catan, and the world conquering military combat of Risk, into a fun easy to play game designed for android tablets. I was on a team of 5 who made this game for our software engineering project in Spring of 2014.

Defend the Herd

Defend the herd won best game at CodeDay Orlando in the summer of 2014. This game was made with Unity 2D and C#, and is a simple fun game, where you get to defend your herd of goats from hungry wolves!

Tron Clone

This tron clone is the first game I made for IOS. I made it using the Cocos2D game library. It is currently broken in the most recent version of Xcode and IOS, but I plan to remake it soon using swift and spritekit! There is a folder in the repo of in game screenshots, so you should definitely look at those.

Work Projects

Haulbox

Haulbox is an IOS application I developed while working at Hatchery Lab. Haulbox is a physical storage by the box solution. The application allows users to be able to order boxes, manage account info, and take pictures of items they place in the boxes for easy retrieval later.

Barpad

Barpad is an IOS application I developed while working at Hatchery Lab. Barpad is an application designed to help bar owners easily take inventory. The app is currently in a beta stage.

Proximity

Proximity is a suite of applications designed around the use of BLE iBeacons.

Kiwee

Kiwee is a food and fitness tracker that is designed for kids age 8-15. Development is currently in early stages, and I will update when more progress is made.

Startups

Meerchat

Meerchat is an IOS application designed to help people meet eachother. It's a local social application in which users take videos to share with the world. I worked with another developer on this project, and my contributions can be seen in detail on my resume.
